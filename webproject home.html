OVERVIEW

Established in the year 1998, the department of Information Technology is offering B.Tech. programme in Information Technology(IT). The programme B.Tech Information Technology mainly deals with installing, maintaining, improving computer systems, operating networks and databases. In short IT deals with application of computing technology to real life processes by creating technical solutions to business problems. The B.Tech. Information Technology programme approved by AICTE, New Delhi and also by ABET, Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology, an International accreditation board, USA.
